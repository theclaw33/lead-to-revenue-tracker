Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Lead to Revenue Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application that integrates with QuickBooks Online.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 QuickBooks Data

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we access:

  • Customer information (names, contact details)
  • Payment records and transaction data
  • Expense records for advertising and marketing categories
  • Invoice data related to payments

2.2 Lead Tracking Data

We collect and process:

  • Lead source information
  • Customer contact details
  • Revenue and payment tracking data
  • Monthly summary and ROI calculations

2.3 Usage Information

We may collect:

  • Application usage logs for troubleshooting
  • Error reports and system performance data
  • Authentication and access tokens

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide lead tracking and revenue analysis services
  • Sync payment data between QuickBooks and your tracking system
  • Generate monthly summary reports and ROI calculations
  • Update advertising spend data automatically
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and improve service quality

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Airtable for lead tracking and summary data
  • Encrypted storage for OAuth tokens and authentication credentials
  • Temporary processing logs that are regularly purged

4.2 Security Measures

We implement:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with QuickBooks
  • Access controls and authentication for all data access
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (bound by confidentiality agreements)

6. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with:

  • QuickBooks Online: Subject to Intuit's privacy policy
  • Airtable: Subject to Airtable's privacy policy
  • Netlify: For hosting and deployment services

Please review these services' privacy policies as they govern data handled by their platforms.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Required to provide our services
  • Necessary to comply with legal obligations
  • Required for legitimate business purposes

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke authorization for QuickBooks integration
  • Export your data in a readable format

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may compromise your personal information, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering the breach and take immediate steps to mitigate the impact.

10. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in the United States. By using our application, you consent to the transfer of your data to the United States.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, providing additional notice.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application's support channels.

14. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws, including but not limited to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and relevant federal regulations.
